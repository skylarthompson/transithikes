[Pinnacle Peak](https://kingcounty.gov/en/dept/dnrp/nature-recreation/parks-recreation/king-county-parks/trails/backcounty-trails/pinnacle-peak) is outside Enumclaw, and is accessible by the [915 DART](https://kingcounty.gov/en/dept/metro/routes-and-service/schedules-and-maps/schedule?route=915) bus from Auburn Station. The bus runs hourly during the week, every 1.5-2 hours on Saturdays, and not at all on Sundays.

Once you get to Enumclaw, get off at the Griffin & Roosevelt stop. Walk to SE 456th St, and then walk south on 276th Ave SE to the Cal Magnusson trailhead, which is at the curve where 276th turns into SE 472nd St. 276th has no sidewalk, but is very low traffic.

There is another trailhead on the south side of the park with a parking lot, but this requires walking along SE Mud Mountain Road for quite a ways, which also has no sidewalk.
